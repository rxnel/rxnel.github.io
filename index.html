About Me

In 2021 I graduated from the University of Maryland, College Park with a B.S. in Computer Science. In July of the same year I started my career as an Implementation Consultant with FAST Enterprises in Olympia, WA. Here I engaged in the continuous support and development of the WA ESD system.

In March 2022, I started working for Leap Orbit as a Full Stack Software Engineer. Here I worked on internal and public facing web-apps that aimed to elegantly reconcile provider health data and health insurance data. During my time here I worked on the main product 'Convergent' a provider data solution for insurance networks to guarantee compliance with the No Surprises Act, ADA/Section 508, and CMS Audits. I also led the project development of the 'CareFinDr' product, a provider search directory that leverages the enriched provider data from Convergent.

In December 2023, I started working for Logitix as a Full Stack Software Engineer. 11 months later, Logitix was acquired by Victory Live in October 2024. I am currently still working for Victory Live.
